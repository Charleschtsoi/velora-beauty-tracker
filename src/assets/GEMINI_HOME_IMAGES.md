# Gemini prompts for Velora home hero

Use these for a **fresh K-beauty** hero that fits the home screen card (rounded corners, vertical crop).

The app loads the banner from **`src/assets/images/home-hero-kbeauty.png`**. Replace that file (keep the same name) or update [`homeHeroImage.ts`](homeHeroImage.ts).

## Hero (primary)

> Vertical mobile app hero, soft K-beauty skincare flat lay on cream marble, pastel mint and blush packaging, glass serum bottles, dewy highlights, bright diffused daylight, minimal luxury, editorial beauty campaign, no text, no logos, no people, clean negative space on one side for UI overlay, 3:4 aspect ratio, photorealistic.

## Alternate (more playful)

> K-beauty vanity desk scene, pastel pink and mint accents, glossy toner bottles, cotton pads, small plant, soft bokeh window light, cute but premium, no text, vertical crop for app header.

## Tips

- Ask for **no text** and **no watermark** so it sits cleanly under typography.
- Export **at least ~1200px** on the short side for sharp display on phones.
