## Demo Flow

1. **Home Screen** - Shows summary cards and recently added products
2. **Inventory** - Full product list with search/filter
3. **Product Detail** - View/edit individual products
4. **Scan Screen** - Camera barcode scanning (with manual entry)
5. **Add Product** - Form to add new products
6. **Notifications** - Expiration alerts grouped by urgency
7. **Categories** - Products organized by category
8. **Settings** - App settings and preferences

## Quick Start

```bash
npx expo start
```

Then scan QR code or press:
- `i` for iOS simulator
- `a` for Android emulator
